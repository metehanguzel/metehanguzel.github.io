Short Bio

I am with the Department of Computer Engineering at ​​College of Engineering and Architecture of Kafkas University since September 2022. Before joining Kafkas University and during my M.Sc and Ph.D., i was employed as a research assistant at the Department of Computer Engineering of Gazi University between September 2013 and September 2022. I received my B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees from Gazi University Department of Computer Engineering respectively in 2012, 2016 and 2022. During my B.Sc. and M.Sc. studies, i studied on Computer Graphics and Image Processing diciplines. Then, in my Ph.D. studies i studied on improving Quality-of-Service in different layers of Internet-of-Things architecture under the supervision of Prof. Suat ÖZDEMİR. The title of my Ph.D. thesis is "IMPROVING QUALITY OF SERVICE IN INTERNET OF THINGS".



My current research interests are Internet-of-Things, IoT Services Computing and Quantum Inspired Computing. Unlike how i look like in my photos, in real life i am a very chill and friendly guy. Do not hesitate to ask me questions about my publications or research.
